Creates widgets that display excerpts from posts or pages in the sidebar.

== Description ==
Creates widgets that display content excerpts or excerpts from posts or pages in the sidebar.
You may use 'more' links, show featured image, set excerpt length of the post or page.

This also adds an excerpt field to your pages like posts have.

You can show content excerpt, the excerpt or both.

Option to highlight the output added.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.

2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

3. Go to your widgets area and you can see the widgets appearing there.

4. Drag drop the widgets to your sidebar and you can see the result.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I show posts/page featured image = 
Yes, there is option to show/hide the post/page featured image.
= Can I select to show the excerpt or a content excerpt = 
Yes, there is an option for each one, that means you can show both!.
